About Us

About the Chef

Mahaz Yvette had a strong passion for baking which became a hobby the older she grew. This hobby soon became a business ideal at the age of 20 and soon the pastisserie was founded in the year 20## early winter. A place where she could be creative and a haven for sweet lovers, she enjoys baking all sorts of treats, hence why the menu items are a variety of cultural items. More specifically, she took her inspirations off of french treats and experimented with other cultural desserts. Her latest pride and joy signature pastry being the "Blueberry Star Cake".

Starting Fresh

As a fresh business with fresh treats always in store, we welcome all visitors of any kind to try the pastries that was inspired by many cultures. We can guarantee that you will enjoy one of the many options we have in store.
